About Just Applause

Just Applause was founded by a 17-year-old who poured the majority of her life savings into a risk that started with nothing more than a late-night TikTok scroll. A simple hashtag — #YOLO — and a song sparked the thought: why not me?

This wasn’t just about fashion. It was about turning a dream into reality against the odds. I thought it through, I struggled with doubt, and I knew failure was a possibility — but I did it anyway.

Even if I didn’t believe in my current self, I did it for my younger self.
